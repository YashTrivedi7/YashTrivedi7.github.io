free time activities

My favorite things to do is cook, eat and do fitness. The last one is required after the second, but thankfully I enjoy doing both. As someone famous once said: "Perfectly balanced, as all things should be." On cold, rainy autumn days and snowy winter days I enjoy sitting down with a warm cup of hot chocolate, a nice book and start reading. Most of my friends are into self development books, which I am not a huge fan of, as in my opinion, most self development book focus on telling you what the right thing to do is, however, story books do a better job at it, as you have a chance to dive in with a characters story, emotionaly invest yourself with it, see all the bad decsions taken and see the consequences it has. I feel this makes you learn more. I love exploring new countries and learn about different cultures. I feel travelling opens a different dimension within you, especially learning how things work in a different country and using those to form new habits to improve your life.
